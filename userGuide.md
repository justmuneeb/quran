# Quranic Text App - User Guide

## About Your App

**Purpose:** Read and study Surah Mulk, Manzil (Fortress), and Surah Yaseen with adjustable text size for comfortable reading on any device.

**Access:** Public - Available on all devices including iOS, Android, and desktop browsers.

## Powered by Manus

This app is built with cutting-edge web technologies: **React 19** for the responsive user interface, **Tailwind CSS 4** for modern styling, and **TypeScript** for type-safe code. The app features auto-scaling infrastructure with global CDN deployment, ensuring fast loading times and smooth performance worldwide.

## Using Your App

### Switching Between Surahs

At the top of the app, you'll see three navigation buttons: **سورة الملك** (Surah Al-Mulk), **المنزل** (Manzil), and **سورة يس** (Surah Yaseen). Click any button to instantly switch between the three sacred texts. The currently selected Surah is highlighted in blue.

### Adjusting Font Size

In the top-right corner of the text area, you'll find font size controls. Click the **minus button (−)** to make text smaller, or the **plus button (+)** to make text larger. The current size displays in the middle (ranging from 12px to 32px). Your preference is automatically saved and will be remembered the next time you visit.

### Reading the Text

Each verse is numbered on the left side for easy reference. Verses are displayed right-to-left in proper Arabic format. Scroll smoothly through all verses of the selected Surah using your device's natural scrolling.

## Managing Your App

You can customize your app through the **Settings** panel in the Management UI. Change the app title, logo, or visibility settings to match your preferences. The **Database** panel lets you view analytics if needed, and the **Domains** panel allows you to bind a custom domain instead of the default manus.space address.

## Next Steps

Talk to Manus AI anytime to request changes or add features. You can enhance the app by adding audio recitations, translations in multiple languages, or bookmarking features. Start exploring the three Surahs and enjoy your reading experience!
